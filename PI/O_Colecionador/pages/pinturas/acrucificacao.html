Title of the work:

The Crucifixion

Artist's name:

Luca Signorelli

Year of the work:

1504

Description:

A work that depicts the moment of the crucifixion of Jesus Christ. Luca Signorelli was one of the main artists of the Tuscan painting school and his work is marked by realistic and expressive details.

Main technique:

The artist uses chiaroscuro, a technique that involves the contrasting use of light and shadow, to create volume and depth in figures and objects. This adds drama and realism to the composition.
